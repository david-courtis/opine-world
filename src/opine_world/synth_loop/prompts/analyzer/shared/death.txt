The level hit game-over. Before retrying, work out WHY from the last frames -- read the actual frame images, not just the ASCII. A game over can be due to various factors, move budget exhaustion, death, bricking the board, etc. Game-over conditions are simple and shared across levels, not a pile of level-specific cases. Issue RESET (action 0) to retry the level with a fresh budget, then avoid repeating the move sequence that exhausted it.
